SCHOOL DETAILS

Date and Time: August ??? to ???, 10AM to 12PM

Cost: $70 per attendee

Location: Cahoon Park, Bay Village Ohio

Each Camper will receive a Summer Soccer School T-shirt on Thursday of camp week.

Goal Keeper Training will take place during the first or second hour each day of training based on age; the second hour of training will be spent with your group in small sided games.

We encourage all Bay Soccer Travel Teams to participate in the August camp prior to the fall season. The emphasis of the camp will be placed on each individual team’s tactics. We will work with the entire age group or a portion of the group, I will do my best to match the age group with their trainer for the up coming season.

FAQ

How much does Dougherty Summer Soccer School Cost?
The cost of the soccer school is $70. If your child cannot make all 4 days of the camp $10 can be deducted for each day missed.
What should my child bring everyday?
Every soccer school attendee should come with a soccer ball (that is the appropriate size for their age group), a water bottle, shinguards, and appropriate clothing for a day a soccer!
Where should we meet?
Doughertys summer soccer school meets at Cahoon Park in Bay Village. Please stop by the brown shed the first day to see where your child should go from there.
